⚡ Features

🔐 x402 Payments

Enterprise-grade payments with on-chain settlement via ERC-402. Credits ledger, trust-based pricing, and automated reconciliation.

🏦 Treasury Management

Real-time balance tracking across USDC, ETH, WETH via Bankr API. Health checks every 5 minutes. Fund management and debt limits.

🤖 Autonomous Agent

Self-sustaining economic agent with trading signals, daily reports, and autonomous loops. Powered by Bankr LLM Gateway.

📊 Reputation System

ERC-8004 identity and reputation integration. Trust score influences pricing and credit limits. Decentralized reputation on-chain.
